Poetry

Selain mengoding saya juga suka membuat Poetry
In the silent night, the whispers of the wind sing,
Silence pleads, in the embrace of darkness,
The caterpillar dances within a circle of sorrow,
Calamity creeps in, spirits fade.

Tasks await in the distant, boundless expanse,
Steps halted in the labyrinth,
Yet behind the curtain of the quiet night,
Butterflies are seen following it.

Vibrations unheard,
But felt in the quiet of the night,
Every passing bird, fear tucked within,
"Soon it will all be over," says the cocoon.
